Your smile’s a sunrise, warm and bright,

your laugh’s a song in soft daylight.

Your eyes, they hold a gentle gleam,

like hopeful stars within a dream.

The way you hold my hand with care,

and brush the strands of my loose hair,

it feels like home, it feels so right—

so safe in your warmth, so soft in your light.
